## 0.0.1

* Initial release of DFTooltip
* Smart tooltip positioning with automatic direction adjustment
* Support for four directions: up, down, left, right
* Auto-hide on scroll functionality
* Flexible width options for different tooltip directions
* Auto-dismiss with configurable duration
* Tap to dismiss functionality
* Works seamlessly in scrollable content
* Overflow protection to keep tooltips on screen
* Customizable background color and border radius

## 0.0.2

* Border support with custom color and width
* Customizable arrow size (height and width)
